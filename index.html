What Families Say

Rachel G
Daughter of Resident
"I am beyond grateful for the wonderful care my mum received during her stay at Woodlands Court Care home, Kirton, Boston,. During her three year stay I visited her frequently and saw for myself how well the staff got to know my mum and understood her individual needs. The staff were very kind and caring, and everything possible was done to help make my mum happy and comfortable, from clean clothes, a clean room and bathroom to delicious home cooked meals and help with personal care, everyone worked so hard. The management and nursing staff are excellent, very approachable, always ready to listen and to help sort out any concerns with sympathy and empathy. As the building is all on ground level my mum was able to walk around safely which helped to keep her active which she enjoyed. Her room, with big patio doors overlooked the pretty garden which she loved, and during her three years there she often said "This is the next best place to home.""
Ben C
Son of Resident
"I'm sure that Woodlands Court is the best care home in the area! Until my recent retirement, I was CEO of a multi-award-winning international company. I understand the hard behind-the-scenes work required to provide such an excellent facility and services. My dad needed full-time care. I researched and assessed 6 care homes and none came close to the standards of Woodlands. Staff are the most important aspect of any business and I am in awe of each and every member of the team at Woodlands Court. Excellence was evident from everybody who cared for my dad. Exceeding the requirement is the norm for these cheerful, diligent and exceptional people who constantly demonstrated that nothing was too much trouble. Spotless, well kept, bright and superbly equipped facilities: an outward sign of the immaculate underpinning management ethos. Please reward this wonderful care home by entrusting your loved family member to their care. Thank you Woodlands for every good day that you gave to my dad!"
